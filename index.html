Frequently Asked Questions

Is WorkFolio completely free to use?
Yes! All core features including the Resume Builder, Portfolio Maker, and ATS Scanner are 100% free with no hidden charges, no ads, and no subscription required.
How does the ATS Scanner work?
It compares your resume text against a job description, calculating a keyword match score using our intelligent algorithm to help you optimize for automated hiring systems.
Is my data saved online?
Your data is securely stored locally in your browser using encrypted localStorage. It is never shared with third parties. Creating an account backs up your resumes for you.
Can I edit my resume after downloading?
Yes! Simply open the Resume Builder again. Your previous data is autosaved, allowing you to edit and re-download as many times as you like.
What file formats does the ATS Scanner support?
The ATS Scanner accepts PDF, DOCX, and TXT files. You can also drag and drop your file directly into the scanner.
