FAQ

Is WorkFolio completely free to use?
Yes! All core features including the Resume Builder, Portfolio Maker, and ATS Scanner are 100% free with no hidden charges.
How does the ATS Scanner work?
It compares your resume text against a job description, calculating a keyword match score to help you optimize for automated hiring systems.
Is my data saved online?
By default, your data is securely stored locally in your browser. It is never shared with third-party advertisers.
Can I edit my resume after downloading?
Yes, simply open the Resume Builder again. Your previous data will be there, allowing you to edit and re-download.
