Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about building your career with WorkFolio.

General
Is WorkFolio completely free?
Yes! WorkFolio is 100% free. You can create unlimited resumes, build portfolios, and use the ATS scanner without any hidden charges, subscriptions, or ads.
Do I need an account to use it?
You can explore the homepage as a guest. However, creating a free account is required to access the Resume Builder, Portfolio Maker, and ATS Scanner — this ensures your work is saved and accessible.
Is my personal data safe?
Your data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. It never leaves your device and is never shared with advertisers. Passwords are hashed with SHA-256 before storage.
Can I use WorkFolio on mobile?
Absolutely. WorkFolio is fully responsive and works on all screen sizes — phone, tablet, and desktop. The Resume Builder and Portfolio Maker both adapt to mobile layouts automatically.
Resume Builder
How do I download my resume as a PDF?
In the Resume Editor, click the "Download PDF" button in the top bar. The resume is captured as a high-resolution PDF. On mobile, use the Download PDF button in the top toolbar.
Can I edit my resume after downloading?
Yes. Simply open the Resume Builder again — your data is autosaved. Make your changes and download a fresh PDF. You can create multiple resume versions for different job applications.
Can I generate a cover letter?
Yes! The Resume Builder has a "Cover Letter" tab. Enter the company name, target role, and optionally paste the job description — the AI generator will write a tailored, professional cover letter for you.
What templates are available?
There are 30+ resume templates ranging from classic executive formats to modern split-column, dark-sidebar, creative, and minimalist designs. You can switch templates at any time without losing your data.
ATS Scanner
How does the ATS Scanner work?
Upload your resume (PDF, DOCX, or TXT) and paste a job description. The scanner uses keyword extraction with synonym mapping to calculate a match score, identifies missing skills, generates interview questions, and gives improvement tips.
What file formats does the ATS Scanner support?
The scanner accepts PDF (parsed via PDF.js), DOCX (via Mammoth.js), and plain TXT files. You can also drag and drop your file directly onto the upload area.
Why is my ATS score low even though I have the skills?
ATS systems match exact keywords. If the job says "JavaScript" and your resume says "JS", it may not match. The scanner includes a synonym map, but it's always best to use the exact terms from the job description in your resume.
Portfolio Maker
What is the AI Co-Pilot in the Portfolio Maker?
The AI Co-Pilot is a natural language command interface inside the editor. Type commands like "switch to dark mode", "make it brutalist", "add a project", "change font to Roboto", or "import my resume" — and the portfolio updates instantly.
Can I export my portfolio as a file?
Yes. Use the "Export" button in the Portfolio Studio to download a complete self-contained HTML file. You can host it on GitHub Pages, Netlify, or any web host for free.
How many portfolio templates are available?
There are 30 distinct portfolio layouts: Bento Grid, Minimalist, Neo-Brutalism, Terminal/Hacker, Glassmorphism, Cyberpunk Neon, Corporate Pro, Split Screen, Marvel/Comic, and many more.
Technical
Why does Chrome show a warning for the portfolio preview?
This was a known issue in earlier versions where the preview iframe used a preview.workfolio.app URL. It has been fixed in the latest version — the preview now uses a workfolio.app preview URL which Chrome recognises as safe.
How do I contact support?
Visit the Contact page from the homepage. Fill in your name, email, and message — it gets sent directly to our team via email. We typically respond within 24 hours.
