Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about building your career with WorkFolio.

Is WorkFolio completely free?
Yes! WorkFolio is currently 100% free. You can create unlimited resumes, build your portfolio, and use the ATS scanner without any hidden charges or subscription fees.
How does the ATS Scanner work?
Our scanner compares the text in your resume against a specific job description you provide. It identifies missing keywords and calculates a "Match Score" to help you optimize your application for automated hiring systems.
Is my personal data safe?
We take privacy seriously. By default, your data is stored locally in your browser (Local Storage). Even if you register, we never share your personal information with third-party advertisers.
Can I edit my resume after downloading?
PDFs are final documents. To make changes, simply open the WorkFolio editor again, update your information, and download a fresh PDF. Your data is saved automatically in the editor.
Can I use this on my mobile phone?
Absolutely. WorkFolio is fully responsive. You can build resumes, update your portfolio, and check your ATS score directly from your smartphone or tablet.
What file formats are supported?
We currently support high-quality *PDF* exports. PDF is the global industry standard for job applications because it preserves your formatting on every device.
Do I need an account to use it?
No, you can use the tools as a Guest. However, creating an account is recommended so your data is backed up safely and accessible if you switch devices or clear your browser cache.
What is a Portfolio Website?
Unlike a standard resume, a Portfolio Website is a digital showcase. It allows you to display project images, detailed case studies, and code snippets to prove your skills visually to recruiters.
Can I upload my existing resume?
We currently do not support file uploads. We recommend copying and pasting your details into our builder. This ensures your data is structured correctly for the highest possible ATS compatibility.
How do I contact support?
If you have specific issues, you can reach us via the "Contact Us" page on the dashboard. We typically respond to queries within 24 hours.
